How to use iConsult?

If you want to:

| Enter a queue

> Register your account here.
>> Visit the spreadsheet file.
>>> Select a slot, then enter your credentials correctly.

| View or monitor a queue

> Simply go to the queue dashboard.
>> Refresh your page to view real-time updates.

If you are a teacher, and you want to create your consultation queue:

> Register your info here.
>> Visit the spreadsheet file.
>>> Open the first sheet, fill up and submit the form there. The system will ask for your account's authorization to run the script in order to submit the form. Simply click 'OK'-> Choose the email account you want to use -> click 'Advanced' -> click 'Go to iConsult_2 (unsafe)' -> finally, click 'Allow'.
>>>> Check if the schedule has been accepted by the system.

Important Reminders:

1. If you are going to run iConsult on your phone, ensure first that you have installed the Google Sheet Application.
2. Make sure to enter your personal information correctly.
3. REGISTER FIRST, before entering/creating a queue. The system will automatically reject your entries if you are not registered.
4. Do not try to claim the already claimed spots in the queue by erasing another student's name and adding yours.
A confirmation email will be sent to the first student to claim the spot, which shall act as a proof incase someone tries to steal their spot.
5. Make sure to attend the schedule of your consultation.
6. Do not forget to update your consultation status (DONE or WAITING).
